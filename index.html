    The Member Ranking only displays through the top 20. Using the Members tab at the top allows you to see all of the team members, but this does not display the member "Ranking". Joe is correct, I don't know that it has ever displayed past the top 20 positions.

    The difference:
    The Member list, includes all of your contributed points
    The Team Member Ranking, limits you to 300 HW Boints, anything more than that has to be Globals... thus changing the order.

    If you have 700 HWBoints and 0 Globals, you will be at the top of the Member list, but you would be in 12th in the Member Rankings (currently using OCF's numbers)
